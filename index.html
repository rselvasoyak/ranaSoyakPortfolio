about me

Hello, my name is Rana Soyak and I am a Web Developer with a passion for making information accessible to all through responsive and user-friendly apps. I bring my experience in retail management and my interest in different cultures to my coding mindset, always prioritizing customer needs and never giving up on learning.

My technical skills include JavaScript, React.js, Firebase, HTML, CSS, SCSS, RESTful APIs,jQuery, and AJAX. I successfully graduated from Juno School of Technology's Web Development Bootcamp with a focus on web accessibility and have completed Accelerated JS Courses. My recent projects include a meditation app and a clone website based off of a design pattern provided by the client. Thank you for considering me for the web developer position.
